I am Adam's Takoyaki

At Adam's Takoyaki, I bring the authentic taste of Japan to your plate! My journey began with a simple love for takoyaki, the iconic Japanese street food that's crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, with bursting flavor. Inspired by the bustling streets of Osaka, I crafted the menu that combines traditional recipes with creative twists, ensuring that every bite is a delightful experience.

Whether you're a long time takoyaki lover or trying it for the first time, I invite you to join us at Adam's Takoyaki and discover the go-to spot for authentic Japanese street food in the Philippines. Can't wait to serve you!
